Studio 360

A streamlined studio management tool

S360 as a tool

  • S360 tool provides a set of streamlined processes to manage all the administrative tasks and day-to-day activities for the members of the studio.
  • It allows the students to create their profile and apply to different programs offered by the Studio and helps them in their journey right from becoming a student in the studio till the completion of their program.
  • It provides the Faculty and Staff of the Studio with tools to handle all of their day-to-day activities, admissions of students, their onboarding and interviewing, managing curriculum and classes, basically everything right from the admission of the student till their graduation more effectively and efficiently.
  • Provides the administrative staff with privileges to override any critical decisions on every user account and help maintain the transparency throughout the studio.
  • Depending on the nature of users accessing the tool, there are 3 specific modules; Student Module, Faculty Module, Staff/Admin Module.

    • S360 Features

    Studio Dashboard

    We like to keep things organised with all your data and information in place so you don't have to navigate through different menus to get where you're headed. Now get all your critical information, tasks, data, and quick links available to you on dashboard.

    User Management

    Manage all the members of the studio in one place. Create users, control their accessibility via permissions, managing their account with full admin overriding capability leaves you on top of your game.

    Admission Center

    Student Application Management with real time updates, efficient onboarding and interviewing processes made life so much easier for you. It keeps you updated, organised, saves your time, effort and also most importantly trees since there is no paperwork involved.

    Tuition Management

    Ability to manage all the tuition fees associated with curriculum and specific to the students based on their choice of program and payment plan. Overriding the payment structure providing full flexibility to cater to the individuals requests.

    Curriculum Designer

    Flexible and customizable curriculum designer provides the admin workforce tools to design classes, courses, programs and workshops offered in a calendar year of the studio. Powered by Clone Curriculum functionality to reuse the existing curriculum in future calendar years.

    Document Manager

    The best part is that all your processes become 100% paperless. You can simply provide documentation like acceptance letters, Completion certificates, International Student documents, Receipts, etc. in the drop box section of each and every student account.

    Publisher

    Keep everyone updated with what is going on in the studio. Publish announcements, notices, events etc. with instant emails and updates on dashboards of the targeted members. This seamless integration with the components like Calendar and Notification center will not let anyone miss out on any crucial information.

    Calendar

    Keeping track of all the events, whether it's an Application Acceptance Period, Enrollment Period, Scheduled Interviews, Scheduled Classes Announcements etc. Our Calendar keeps it organized for you, providing the user with an overview of their day, week and month.

    Notification Center

    We offer a well-planned reminder system so you're able to deliver the right message at the right time. Whether it's a reminder about deadlines, processing payments, or even rolling out an important message to studio members, we send it all across.

    Reporting

    We know you want to preserve the data and work on getting insights from them. Reporting center keeps all the historical data and inform the users with the latest trends. Users can request for customized reports to get the data presented in a dashboard format.

    Attendance & Performance

    Ability to record the attendance and performance for each and every student in the class ensures record keeping and transparency between all the members of the studio.

    Materials

    Faculty and Staff can provide any kind of course materials like syllabus, reading materials, books in multitude of formats like PDF, PPTs, Word Docs, Video links etc. to the students by simply uploading them in the dedicated Materials section of the class.
