Namastey!

I'm a filmmaker, engineer, and designer who thrives at the intersection of storytelling and technology. My passion lies in crafting visually compelling narratives — whether through directing, editing, or collaborating with creative teams to bring ideas to life on screen.

With a strong foundation in visual storytelling and a sharp eye for detail, I specialize in photography, cinematography, video editing, and post-production. I’m skilled in tools like Adobe Light Room, Adobe Premiere Pro and DaVinci Resolve, and I work closely with creative directors to shape stories that resonate.

My background in UI/UX design and technical project management adds a unique edge to my process, allowing me to bridge the gap between creativity and precision. Whether I’m behind the camera or shaping the final cut, I’m driven by the power of story — and the craft it takes to tell it well.

Photography Services in New York & New Jersey

Looking for a professional photographer in Jersey City, New York, or anywhere across New Jersey? I’m the one — a visual storyteller specializing in cinematic photography for events, family photography, engagement sessions, portraits, birthdays, baby showers, and behind-the-scenes storytelling.

Whether you’re planning a birthday celebration in Hoboken, a music video shoot in Brooklyn, or a film in Manhattan, I bring a creative, detail-oriented approach to every project. My work blends emotion and atmosphere, capturing authentic moments with a cinematic edge. With years of experience and a deep love for visual storytelling, I bring both technical skill and a warm, personal approach to every session.

My goal is to create images that feel intentional, timeless, and true to you. I especially enjoy working with children and families, and have extensive experience photographing children with Autism and other special needs— offering a patient, thoughtful experience for every family.
